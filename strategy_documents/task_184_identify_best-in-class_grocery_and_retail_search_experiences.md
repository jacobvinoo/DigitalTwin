## Search Experience Principles
- Personalization: Tailoring search results to individual user preferences.
- Real-time updates: Ensuring search results reflect current inventory and availability.
- Accessibility: Incorporating diverse search methods like voice and visual search.
- Efficiency: Providing robust filtering and sorting options to streamline the search process.
