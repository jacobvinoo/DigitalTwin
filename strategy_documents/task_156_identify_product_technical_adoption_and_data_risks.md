# Detailed Strategy Document: Identify product, technical, adoption, and data risks

## Strategic Question
How do we win in this market?

## Market Context
The market involves enhancing the supermarket search experience through the implementation of Algolia to improve search relevance, customer discovery, and conversion rates.

## Competitor Insights
- Competitors are leveraging AI-driven search algorithms to enhance user experience.
- Some supermarkets have integrated personalized search results based on user behavior.
- Competitors are focusing on mobile-first search experiences.

## Recommended Strategic Position
Position as a leader in personalized and highly relevant supermarket search experiences by leveraging advanced AI and mobile-first strategies.

## Strategic Options Evaluated
- Implement advanced AI algorithms to improve search relevance.
- Focus on personalized search experiences to increase customer engagement.
- Enhance mobile search capabilities to capture on-the-go users.

## 30/60/90 Day Execution Plan
### 30-Day Plan (Phase 1: Setup & Alignment)
- Focus action: Conduct a feasibility study on integrating Algolia with current systems.
- Establish search catalog sync and index configuration.
- Align technical stakeholders on success indicators.

### 60-Day Plan (Phase 2: Integration & Beta Launch)
- Focus action: Perform a cost-benefit analysis of AI-driven search improvements.
- Implement search layout widgets and facet filters in development components.
- Launch internal beta to evaluate search responsiveness and accuracy.

### 90-Day Plan (Phase 3: Launch & Scale)
- Focus action: Engage with a focus group to understand customer preferences in search functionalities.
- Go live with production indexes and scale traffic.
- Monitor search click-through analytics and optimize relevancy filters.

## Risk Assessment & Mitigations
- Technical challenges in integrating Algolia with existing systems.
- Data privacy concerns with personalized search results.
- Adoption resistance from users accustomed to current search functionalities.
- Potential high costs associated with advanced AI implementation.

## Key Assumptions
- Algolia can be effectively integrated with current supermarket systems.
- Customers value personalized and relevant search experiences.
- Investment in AI will lead to a significant increase in search-led conversions.

## Key Decisions Required
Decide on the level of investment in AI technologies for search enhancement.

- Market research reports on AI in retail search.
- Case studies of successful Algolia implementations in retail.
- Customer feedback surveys on search experience.
